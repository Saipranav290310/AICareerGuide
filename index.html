Emiratization Guide

Understand key careers prioritized for UAE nationals

What is Emiratization?

Emiratization is a strategic initiative by the UAE government to increase the number of Emirati citizens employed in both the public and private sectors. It aims to empower and integrate national talent into the workforce, ensuring sustainable economic development and preserving national identity.

Prioritized Sectors and Careers for UAE Nationals

Banking & Finance

Roles in financial analysis, banking operations, investment management.

Technology & AI

Cybersecurity specialists, data scientists, AI engineers, IT management.

Healthcare

Doctors, nurses, healthcare administrators, medical researchers.

Education

Teachers, educational leaders, curriculum developers.

Government & Public Sector

Policy makers, public administrators, strategic planning roles.

Energy & Sustainability

Renewable energy engineers, environmental scientists, sustainable development roles.

About AI Career Guide

Revolutionizing career guidance for UAE students

Our Mission

To empower UAE students with AI-driven career guidance that aligns their unique talents and interests with opportunities in the UAE's evolving job market. This project is specifically designed to support students in Grades 9-12.

How it Works: Your Journey

1. Profile Setup: Tell us about your academic background, interests, and career goals.

2. Assessment: Our AI generates a personality profile and key strengths based on your inputs.

3. AI Recommendations: Get personalized career and course suggestions tailored to you.

4. Universities: Explore top UAE universities and their programs.

5. Report: Download a comprehensive PDF report summarizing your profile and recommendations.

6. AI Assistant: Ask our chatbot any career-related questions.
